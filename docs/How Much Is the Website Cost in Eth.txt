2, What Is the Real Website Cost in Ethiopia?
The website cost in Ethiopia varies depending on the level of quality and functionality required. Generally, pricing falls into three main categories:

Basic Websites (15,000 – 30,000 ETB)
Basic websites are suitable for small businesses and individuals who need a simple online presence.

They typically include:

Essential pages (Home, About, Services, Contact)
Basic design layout
Mobile-friendly structure
While affordable, these websites are best suited for businesses with limited requirements.

Standard Business Websites (30,000 – 80,000 ETB)
These websites are designed for businesses that want to build credibility and compete effectively online.

They include:

Custom design aligned with brand identity
SEO-friendly structure
Optimized performance and responsiveness
Improved user experience
Most growing businesses in Ethiopia fall into this category.

Advanced / Custom Websites (80,000 – 200,000+ ETB)
Advanced websites are built for scalability, performance, and complex functionality.

They may include:

E-commerce systems
Booking platforms
Custom dashboards
API integrations
These solutions are ideal for larger businesses and digital platforms.
